About

Who can apply
Bachelor's, Master's, PhD students and industry persons with computer science, electrical, maths or any other domain related to machine learning and artificial intelligence backgrounds in any year of their study are eligible to apply for this program. It will be held in virtual format.
Experience needed
Participation does not require any previous research experience. Still, having a background in programming languages such as C/C++/Python/Java and completing introductory computer science coursework can be advantageous.
Workshop activities
During this online workshop, participants will be paired with one or two faculty mentors who will provide continuous guidance throughout the program and beyond. The workshop will cover a wide range of research topics, including a foundational understanding of AI, privacy, and zero-touch networks, as well as insights into the current state of the art. Participants will collaborate with faculty on existing laboratory projects and have the opportunity to initiate new independent research initiatives. Upon completing the online sessions, participants can showcase their work through virtual poster presentations and engage in interactive discussions. They will also have access to online resources and research laboratory facilities, allowing for hands-on activities in a digital environment. Additionally, there is potential for extending and publishing these projects as short or full papers, providing a valuable opportunity for participants to contribute to the academic community.
